Our Story

As a family-owned establishment, we are super proud to be able to be a part of growing the CrossFit community in Australia. Since we opened our doors in October 2018 a multitude of fitness levels and abilities have joined the gym’s community. All training hard, achieving goals, making new ones, eating and sleeping better, being part of a tight-knit community of support – this is CrossFit. Come up for try us out! Sonia & Larry

Community

The sense of community is one of the special features of CrossFit. Community at CrossFit is unique; everyone knows one another, there is always someone to help with technique, you compare WOD results, discuss competitions etc. The last person is as important as the first to finish the workout cheered on to the end. Working out within our CrossFit community makes us feel good and encourages us to push ourselves to new limits that we didn’t think were possible. We welcome new members and make them feel that they are part of this vibe.
